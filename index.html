WHO ARE WE

At Rise Initiative, we believe every child deserves the chance to dream, learn, and build a brighter future. We are a community-driven NGO dedicated to providing access to quality education and opportunities for underprivileged children. Through mentorship, resources, and support, we aim to empower young minds to rise above barriers and unlock their true potential.
